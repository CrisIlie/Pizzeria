About us

Pizzeria - Pizza che ride

Bringing smiles since we know each other! We are a pizzeria that always has a smile on its face (you know our logo!). And which one comes to you next when you get together with friends and laugh at the memories, when it's a colleague's birthday at the office and you laugh at kitten videos or when you want to at least bring a smile to your significant other's face and you order a surprise pizza.

So from now on, every time you order from Pizzeria, remember that: For a happier life, you need to laugh more!
